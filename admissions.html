Why Montessori is Better Than a Traditional Daycare?

What’s more important for children - learning and growing at their own pace, or living on the basis of adult-led schedules? What they need most is a calm, orderly environment where trained professionals help them develop as individuals, in the most enjoyable way. Here are a few ways in which The Freethinking School can transform the way you approach childcare, so anxious parents can rest assured.

The Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2017

The Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2017 received the approval of the President of India in March 2017. The related Maternity Benefit Bill was passed by both houses of the parliament in the same month.

As per the Act, paid maternity leave has been revised to 26 weeks from 12 weeks earlier. Additionally, the new law mandates that companies with more than 50 employees must provide a Daycare or Crèche facility within an acceptable distance.




Onsite Montessori Facility

An exclusive facility set up on or near premises of the corporate. Fit out and setup costs borne by corporate.

    Advantages

  • Easy access for employees,
  • Scalable operational costs,
  • Complete corporate control on design, access & quality.
Near-site Montessori Facility

TFS sets up a near-site facility for the corporate with an agreed-upon number of seats blocked for the partner.

    Advantages

  • Upfront costs borne by TFS, no upfront CAPEX for corporate,
  • Easy access for employees and scalable operations.
Underwritten Subsidized Childcare

Corporate partner blocks required number of seats across TFS locations by paying the admission fee and monthly tuition fee.

    Advantages

  • Can be implemented with immediate effect,
  • No CAPEX obligations on Corporate,
  • Testing ground for setting up an onsite center,

FAQ




At The Freethinking School, we encourage your curiosity to understand our methodology better. Please do feel free to call or write to us about any query you may have regarding your child's formative education.

Who can join the After School programs at TFS?
Children between the age group of 2.5 to 16 years are eligible to join this program. The TED-ED Club is open to children 8 to 18 years.
What are the timings?
The After School Care program starts at 12.30pm and ends at 5.30pm. The After School Club programs will end latest by 7.00pm.
What are the different programs?
Our programs are broadly divided into After School Care, After School Club and the TED-Ed Club. The After School Care program is a gift for the child and a boon to working parents. While the After School Club will offer a mix of structured and unstructured individual activities from which children can choose from. TED-Ed Club is a part of TED’s effort to celebrate youth ideas in the form of TED style talks.
What are the individual activities on offer?
We plan to offer theatre, music, baking, languages etc. Please check the calendar for the current list of activities on offer.
Do you introduce new activities?
Yes, we keep offering new activities based on demand or the needs of the child. Log on to our FB page to know more.
What is your student /teacher ratio?
Varies from activity to activity.
Does the fees include snacks and lunch?
No. These come at an extra cost. Kindly fill in the request form by clicking the snack icon on this page.
Do you have transport?
Yes, kindly fill in the request form by clicking the transport icon on this page.
What are the safety measures implemented at the school?
We have 24 hour security guards, CCTV cameras, full time attenders and our ever-attentive teachers.
