TFS @ Koramangala

TFS@Koramangala is the first centre and also the head office of The Freethinking School in India.
Strategically located in 3rd Block on the Service Road, this highly visible center is equipped with a separate Toddler and Pre-primary environment,
creative and reading zones, kitchen garden, an outdoor play area,
fully equipped kitchen and an activity room.

Programs Offered




    • Montessori - Toddler(M0 - 1.5 to 2.5years)
    • pre-primary (M1,2,3 -2.5 to 6 years)
    • after school care(2.5 to 12 years)
    • weekend workshops

TFS @ Kochi

Nestled within a 10-acre campus TFS@Kochi is a 10,000sft spacious environment located next to Infopark at Kakkanad.
The centre is equipped with separate Toddler and Pre-primary environments, library room, science room, music and activity room,
art and craft room, a large child-specific kitchen and snack room, creative zones, aquariums, nap areas, kitchen gardens,
large outdoor and indoor play areas and an Amphitheatre.

Programs Offered




    • Montessori - Toddler(M0 - 1.5 to 2.5years)
    • pre-primary (M1,2,3 -2.5 to 6 years)
    • after school care(2.5 to 12 years)
    • weekend workshops

TFS @ Kudlu

Strategically located right next to a large number of residential apartments, TFS@Kudlu also overlooks the beautiful Haralur Lake.
The centre is spread over 8000sft of learning spaces including a large outdoor play area. The 2 floors are divided into separate Toddler and Pre- primary environments,
fully equipped library, art and reading corners, child-specific kitchens and snack rooms, creative zones, aquariums, kitchen gardens, nap areas and a large activity room.

Programs Offered




    • Montessori - Toddler(M0 - 1.5 to 2.5years)
    • pre-primary (M1,2,3 -2.5 to 6 years)
    • after school care(2.5 to 12 years)
    • weekend workshops(parents and children)
