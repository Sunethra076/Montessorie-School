After School Care Program




The highly trained and enthusiastic faculty at the school will put the children’s time to productive use with fun-filled learning exercises such as reading, building blocks, puzzles, art, craft, and much more. Children are encouraged to read books appropriate for their reading level from the library available at the campus or bring books of their own choice. The program will also include snack and nap time for the young ones. As an additional service, we also offer the children professional help in doing their homework.

Program Details:

  • Age group: 2 - 12 years
  • Monday to Friday
  • Timing: 12:30 PM to 6:00 PM
  • Monthly & Daily plans available
  • Activities include:
    • Art
    • Craft
    • Sports
    • Building toys and puzzles
    • Cooking
    • Music
    • Snacks
    • Nap time
    • Quiet reading time
    • Free play




After School Club Program




The After School Club at The Freethinking School will provide a program that exposes students to diverse, creative and challenging activities that enhance physical, intellectual, emotional, moral and social development. The program will offer a mix of structured and unstructured activities including team, small group and individual, outdoor and indoor activities.

Activities:

    • Music(western)
    • Dance(western)
    • Theatre
    • Drawing, Sketching & Painting
    • Baking(children/Adults)
    • Vocal training and more
